Audition Process

Students who wish to audition for TMEA All State Orchestra must first audition for region orchestra. Eligible students may then record at Area to audition for All State Orchestra. Candidates for Area Recording will be selected by Region audition ranking, using a straight rank. Please refer to the Region 18 Orchestra Handbook for more details, but recording quotas for Region 18 are as follows:
